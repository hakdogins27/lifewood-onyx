Who We Are

A global partner dedicated to delivering data excellence for AI development and historical preservation.

Our Story

Lifewood was established to address a critical need in the AI industry: access to reliable, high-quality data. We recognized that even the most advanced algorithms are only as effective as the data they are trained on. Our journey began with a commitment to building a global network of experts and a robust technological platform to collect, label, and organize data with unmatched precision and scale.

Our Approach

We combine human expertise with advanced technology to deliver superior data solutions. Our proactive approach means we work as an extension of your team, adapting to your project's unique requirements and evolving with your needs. We believe in purposeful speed, ensuring timely delivery without ever compromising on the quality that defines our work.

Our History of Excellence

Over two decades of innovation and trust.

2004

Lifewood is founded with a mission to digitize historical and genealogical records.

2009

Expanded operations globally, opening our first secure facility in the Philippines.

2018

Achieved SOC 2 Type 2 certification, affirming our commitment to enterprise-level security.

Today

A trusted data partner for leading AI innovators and the world's largest genealogy platforms.

Global Operations, Uncompromising Security

Our operational footprint spans the globe, with state-of-the-art, secure facilities designed for the most demanding data projects. This allows us to offer 24/7 workflows and access to a diverse, multilingual workforce while maintaining the highest levels of data privacy and security.

Every Lifewood facility is SOC 2 certified and adheres to strict, internationally recognized security protocols. Whether we are handling sensitive corporate documents or irreplaceable historical archives, our commitment to protecting your data is absolute.
